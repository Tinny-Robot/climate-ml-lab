Key Data Insights

Weather data for Abeokuta, Ogun State, reveals a generally warm and humid climate with average temperature around 26.88°C and humidity at 78.57%. Wind speed is typically mild (7.39 m/s), and daily precipitation averages 2.89 mm, with occasional heavy rainfall.

Seasonal patterns are prominent: temperature and wind speed vary across months, with distinct wet and dry seasons impacting precipitation significantly. Strong day-to-day continuity is observed in weather patterns, useful for short-term forecasts.

Key relationships include an inverse correlation between temperature and humidity, and a direct link between higher humidity and increased precipitation. Surface pressure tends to be consistent throughout the year, with minor fluctuations.
